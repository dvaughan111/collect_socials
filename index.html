Troubleshooting

If the widget doesn't appear in your form:

  • Ensure you've saved the widget properly
  • Check that the widget is enabled in your form
  • Verify that the image URLs are accessible
  • Clear your browser cache and refresh the form editor
  • Try using the SVG fallback for the OnlyFans logo if the PNG isn't loading
